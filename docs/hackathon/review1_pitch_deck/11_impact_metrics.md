### Real-World Scenarios:

**Scenario 1: Smallholder in Bihar**
- **Before:** Grows wheat like everyone else, no access to scheme info
- **With KisanSetu:** Learns he's eligible for PMFBY (crop insurance), gets satellite alert about water stress, switches to drought-resistant variety
- **Result:** ₹35,000 additional income + insurance protection

**Scenario 2: Woman Farmer in Maharashtra**
- **Before:** Cannot read English apps, depends on husband for market info
- **With KisanSetu:** Uses voice assistant in Marathi, gets price forecasts, knows best selling time
- **Result:** Independent decision-making, 18% better prices

**Scenario 3: Progressive Farmer in Punjab**
- **Before:** Manual monitoring, reactive pest management
- **With KisanSetu:** Weekly satellite health reports, early disease warnings, precision fertilizer application
- **Result:** 22% yield increase, 30% reduction in pesticide use
